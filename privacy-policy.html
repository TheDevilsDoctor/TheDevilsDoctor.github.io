Welcome to Snarky Weather. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect the following types of information:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide weather updates tailored to your location.

We may use third-party services such as weather data providers and analytics tools that may collect data in accordance with their privacy policies. These services may include but are not limited to:

5. Data Retention & Security

We do not store personal data beyond what is necessary for the App's functionality. Location data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers. We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Local Processing

As of the current state of the application, no servers are being used, and everything runs entirely on the device. All data processing happens locally, ensuring that no personal data is transmitted to external servers.
